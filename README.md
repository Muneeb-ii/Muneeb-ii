Born and raised in Karachi. I enjoy building things, from software and research tools to entirely new ideas. Lately, I've been interested in AI, quantitative finance, and understanding how information and human behavior shape markets.

## Now

- Research Assistant at the HUMANE Lab
- Data & ML Intern at Prospector, exploring relationships between mining news, market data, and commodity behavior
- Building Precode, plus a few ML and quant projects on the side

## Projects

**MultiSOCIAL Toolbox** &nbsp; Open-source software for analyzing human interaction across text, audio, and video. I worked on cross-platform packaging, speech pipelines, and pose estimation. Paper submitted.

**Maine Lakes Dashboard** &nbsp; Predicts lake water clarity from environmental and chemical data using CatBoost and random forests, with explainability so the output isn't a black box.

**Quant research** &nbsp; Ongoing work in market modeling, regime detection, portfolio construction, and systematic trading strategies.

**Precode** &nbsp; A pre-coding layer for AI-assisted development. It produces the structured docs and specs a project needs before the first line of code, which is usually the step people skip.

**FinTrack** &nbsp; A personal finance tool for tracking and understanding spending, with analytics and an AI layer on top.
